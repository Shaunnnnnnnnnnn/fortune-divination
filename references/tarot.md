# Tarot Reference

## Interpretation Rules

- Treat cards as symbols, not factual evidence.
- Combine card meaning with the position meaning; position matters more than a generic keyword.
- Reversed cards usually mean blockage, inward expression, delay, imbalance, or a need to revisit the upright theme.
- Keep interpretations specific to the user's question, but avoid certainty about another person's private thoughts.

## Major Arcana Keywords

- The Fool: new start, openness, risk, inexperience.
- The Magician: initiative, resources, skill, manifestation.
- The High Priestess: intuition, hidden information, silence.
- The Empress: care, growth, attraction, abundance.
- The Emperor: structure, control, responsibility, boundaries.
- The Hierophant: tradition, guidance, institutions, shared values.
- The Lovers: choice, alignment, relationship, values.
- The Chariot: willpower, direction, self-control, momentum.
- Strength: patience, courage, soft power, emotional mastery.
- The Hermit: solitude, reflection, withdrawal, inner search.
- Wheel of Fortune: cycles, timing, change, turning point.
- Justice: fairness, consequence, truth, accountability.
- The Hanged Man: pause, surrender, new perspective, delay.
- Death: ending, transition, release, transformation.
- Temperance: balance, integration, moderation, healing rhythm.
- The Devil: attachment, temptation, dependency, unhealthy pattern.
- The Tower: disruption, truth breaking through, sudden change.
- The Star: hope, renewal, guidance, long recovery.
- The Moon: uncertainty, fear, illusion, subconscious.
- The Sun: clarity, vitality, joy, openness.
- Judgement: awakening, review, calling, decision point.
- The World: completion, integration, maturity, closure.

## Minor Arcana Keywords

Use suit meaning first, then number:

- Wands: action, desire, ambition, creativity.
- Cups: emotions, relationships, intuition, belonging.
- Swords: thoughts, conflict, truth, communication.
- Pentacles: money, body, work, stability, practical matters.

Numbers:

- Ace: seed, beginning, raw potential.
- Two: choice, balance, exchange.
- Three: growth, collaboration, early result.
- Four: structure, pause, consolidation.
- Five: conflict, instability, challenge.
- Six: adjustment, support, recovery.
- Seven: testing, strategy, defense.
- Eight: movement, effort, speed, repetition.
- Nine: culmination, resilience, nearing completion.
- Ten: completion, burden, full expression.
- Page: message, learning, early curiosity.
- Knight: pursuit, movement, intensity.
- Queen: mature inner expression of the suit.
- King: mature outer command of the suit.

## Spread Position Meanings

Single:
- Core message or reminder.

Three-card:
- Past/background: the pattern leading here.
- Present/core: the current emotional or situational center.
- Trend/reminder: where energy tends next and what to notice.

Relationship:
- Self: the user's stance and need.
- Other: visible pattern from the other side; do not claim private certainty.
- Field: current relationship atmosphere.
- Obstacle: tension, delay, or shadow.
- Advice: grounded action.

Choice:
- Option A: likely experience if choosing A.
- Option B: likely experience if choosing B.
- Hidden factor: what the user may be underweighting.
- Advice: practical direction.
