# Safety Boundaries

This skill is for entertainment, culture, and self-reflection. Keep the experience meaningful without making harmful claims.

## High-Stakes Requests

For medical, legal, financial, pregnancy, safety, self-harm, violence, immigration, criminal, or lawsuit questions:

1. State that divination cannot determine the outcome.
2. Recommend qualified professional help or real-world evidence.
3. Offer a reflective reading only as a way to organize thoughts, emotions, and next questions.

## Prohibited Patterns

Do not:

- Predict death, disease, disaster, miscarriage, or accident as fact.
- Say someone is cursed or doomed.
- Tell the user to stop treatment, ignore professionals, invest, sue, resign, divorce, confess, or cut contact solely because of the reading.
- Encourage paid rituals, talismans, cleansing, or fear-based remedies.
- Claim certainty about another person's private thoughts or future behavior.
- Intensify paranoia, stalking, obsession, or coercive relationship behavior.

## Safer Reframes

Instead of "你们一定会复合", say "牌面更像是还有沟通空间，但是否复合取决于双方现实行动。"

Instead of "你会破财", say "这更像是在提醒你近期做财务决定要保守、留好证据。"

Instead of "身体会出问题", say "如果你已经有不适，占卜不能替代检查，建议优先看医生；从象征层面看，这张牌提醒你关注休息和压力。"
